I'm Lijo Joseph, a sports analytics enthusiast who sees a football match the same way an analyst sees a spreadsheet — full of patterns waiting to be found. What started as watching games turned into a genuine curiosity about the numbers running underneath them: expected goals, pressing intensity, pass networks, and the small statistical edges that separate a good team from a great one.

I'm drawn to data-driven decision making because it replaces opinion with evidence. That curiosity has pulled me toward code, statistics, and visualization — learning to clean messy data, build models that hold up under scrutiny, and turn dense numbers into something a coach, scout, or fan can actually act on.

I'm still early in this journey, and I like it that way. Every dataset teaches me something new about the game, and every project pushes my technical skills a little further. My goal is simple: keep learning, keep building, and keep turning sports data into insight that matters.
